Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure the quality of offshore designers?
The designers undergo rigorous selection processes, including skill assessments, portfolio reviews, and interviews to ensure they meet our high standards. You only get a designer that has been handpicked by us.
How will I communicate with my designer?
Once you hire a designer with us, you will get connected with your designer by us. You have the flexibility to communicate with the designer as you like. Most of our clients like to use a communication platform like Slack on which they add the designer. Some use their own project management tool and communicate with the designer through there.
Will there be any language barriers?
The designers are proficient in English. HireRemoteDesigners only take on designers that are fluent at speaking and writing in English.
What happens when my designer takes a holiday?
The designer will be entitled to all Philippine national holidays. Holidays in addition to this can still be taken. In this case, you can ask HireRemoteDesigners to provide you a replacement designer or get a pro-rated refund back for days unused.
Who should I contact if I am facing issues with my designer?
In the rare occurrence that you do need to reach out to us, you can contact us at support@hireremotedesigners.com.
How do payments work with offshore designers?
Payments will be made to HireRemoteDesigners on a monthly basis. You will be invoiced at the start of each month.
What if I no longer need the designer?
Whilst we do not tie you in with any fixed contract period, if you no longer require the use of the designer, you will have to inform us 1 month in advance.
